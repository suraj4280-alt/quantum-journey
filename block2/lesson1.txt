STATEVECTOR

Statevector is the mathematical representation
of a quantum state.

For one qubit:

|ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩

Statevector

[α, β]

The amplitudes determine the quantum state.

Measurement probabilities are:

P(0)=|α|²

P(1)=|β|²

Examples

Default state

|0⟩

↓

[1,0]


After X

|1⟩

↓

[0,1]


After H

|+⟩

↓

[1/√2,1/√2]


After X then H

|−⟩

↓

[1/√2,-1/√2]
